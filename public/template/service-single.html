What's Covered in Our Refrigerator Repair Service

Whether your fridge is running warm, leaking water, making strange noises, or simply won't turn on — our technicians have seen it all. We run a full diagnostic before quoting any repair, so you know exactly what's wrong and what it will cost.

Compressor & Cooling System

We diagnose compressor faults, refrigerant leaks, and condenser coil failures that cause your fridge to stop cooling.

Defrost & Ice Maker

From heavy frost buildup to an ice maker that won't produce ice, we repair the full defrost system and ice maker assembly.

Door Seals & Gaskets

A worn door seal lets warm air in and forces the compressor to overwork. We replace gaskets on all fridge and freezer models.

Electronic Control Boards

Control board failures can cause erratic temperatures, display errors, or a fridge that won't respond. We source and replace boards for all major brands.
